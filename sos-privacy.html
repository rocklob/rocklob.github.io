Privacy Policy for Sound-o-Scope

Last Updated: 2026/01/03

This privacy policy governs your use of the software application Sound-o-Scope ("Application") for mobile devices.

What information does the Application obtain and how is it used?

The Application requires access to your device's microphone (android.permission.RECORD_AUDIO) for its core functionality, which is to visualize audio in real-time.

The audio data captured from the microphone is processed ephemerally on your device to create the oscilloscope visualization.

We do not store, save, record, or transmit any audio data. The data is processed in real-time and is immediately discarded. We do not share this information with any third parties.
